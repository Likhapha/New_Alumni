Business Information Technology Programmes at LUCT

The following qualifications are available in Business Information Technology. Please consult the webpages for BSC in Business Information Technology and Diploma in Business Information Technology for more detailed descriptions, admission requirements and Career Opportunities

Postgraduate Qualifications

  • Bachelor of Science in Business Information Technology.
  • Diploma in Multimedia and Business Information Technology

Project Management Institute (PMI):

PMI is a global professional association for project management. It offers certifications such as Project Management Professional (PMP) and resources for project management professionals.

Institute of Information Technology Professionals South Africa (IITPSA):

IIBA is a professional association dedicated to supporting business analysts. It offers certifications, resources, and networking opportunities for business analysis professionals.

Association of Information Technology Professionals (AITP):

AITP is a professional association focused on advancing the IT profession through professional development, networking, and advocacy.

Information Systems Audit and Control Association (ISACA):

ISACA is a global association that provides knowledge, certifications, and community for information systems audit, assurance, security, risk, privacy, and governance professionals.

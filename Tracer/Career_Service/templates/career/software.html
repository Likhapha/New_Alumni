Computer Society of Lesotho (CSL):

CSL aims to advance computing as a science and profession in Lesotho. It provides a platform for professionals, academics, and students to network, share knowledge, and collaborate on projects related to computing and technology.

Institute of Information Technology Professionals South Africa (IITPSA):

The IITPSA is a South African professional body for IT professionals. It provides networking opportunities, professional development, and industry recognition

International Association of Computer Science and Information Technology (IACSIT):

IACSIT is a registered international scientific association of distinguished scholars engaged in Computer Science and Information Technology. The IACSIT members include research and development center heads, faculty deans, department heads, professors, research scientists, engineers, scholars, experienced software development directors, managers and engineers, university postgraduate and undergraduate engineering and technology students, etc.

Association for Computing Machinery (ACM):

ACM is the world's largest computing society, serving professionals and students in the computing field. It offers access to cutting-edge research, publications, conferences, and special interest groups covering various aspects of computing, including software engineering.

Computer Weekly

Computer Weekly provides industry updates, expert opinions, case studies, and in-depth articles to keep professionals informed about the latest trends and developments in the technology sector.

Software Engineering Daily

The Software Engineering Daily podcast provides daily interviews and discussions with software engineers, developers, and industry experts. It covers a wide range of topics related to software engineering, including technology trends, programming languages, and career advice.

PNet

PNet is a leading job portal that connects job seekers with employers across various industries in South Africa and other African countries, including Lesotho

Indeed

Indeed is a popular job search website that aggregates job listings from various sources, including company websites, job boards, and recruitment agencies.

Lesotho Government Portal

Lesotho Government Portal - Public Service Recruitment: The official website of the Government of Lesotho often advertises job vacancies in various sectors, including government departments and agencies.

LinkedIn Jobs - Lesotho

LinkedIn is a professional networking platform where companies often post job vacancies. You can search for jobs using relevant keywords and filters, as well as connect with recruiters and professionals in your industry.
